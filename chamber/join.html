Join the Chamber

Complete this application to join the Ogden Valley Chamber of Commerce and choose the membership level that best fits your organization.

Membership Level

Membership Levels

NP Membership

$0 / year · Non-profit only

Perfect for registered non-profit organizations that want a voice in the community.

Bronze Membership

$150 / year

Great for new businesses looking for basic visibility and networking.

Silver Membership

$300 / year

Ideal for growing businesses wanting more promotion and training.

Gold Membership

$600 / year

Maximum exposure, sponsorships, and leadership opportunities.

NP Membership Benefits

  • No annual membership fee for qualified non-profits.
  • Listing in the online chamber directory.
  • Access to quarterly nonprofit round-table.
  • Discounted tickets for chamber trainings.

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • All NP benefits.
  • Ribbon-cutting ceremony for grand openings.
  • Basic social media shout-outs.
  • Access to member-only job board.

Silver Membership Benefits

  • All Bronze benefits.
  • Priority placement in the chamber directory.
  • Two spotlight features on the home page per year.
  • Invitations to leadership luncheons.

Gold Membership Benefits

  • All Silver benefits.
  • Logo on chamber home page year-round.
  • Sponsorship opportunities for major events.
  • Custom marketing consult with chamber staff.
